Tour Our Factory!

NOTE: We care about the health of our customers and fellow Spookers. Our factory tours have been cancelled due to COVID-19, and all of our efforts are concentrated on bringing you safe Spookies this holiday season. We look forward to seeing you again soon!



Directions:

1. Head toward Deception Pass Bridge. Underneath it you will find a gnome guarding the entrance to the Cookie Cave.
2. Promise him Spookies on your way back - he will let you through.
3. Once through the Cookie Cave take 150 steps north, then 250 steps east through the Wicked Woods.
4. Watch out for the werewolves.
5. Continue east until you see Maleficent Mountain. Climb to the top and grab the shiny key with the emerald jewel shaped like a lollipop.
6. Descend from the mountain and look for the Hobbit-like door in the tree nearby.
7. Use the key to unlock ... you made it!
